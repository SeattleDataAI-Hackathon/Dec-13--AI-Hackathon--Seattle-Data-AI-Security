DENIAL REASON: Medical Necessity Not Met

Your claim has been denied because the requested MRI of the lumbar spine does not meet our medical necessity criteria. According to our clinical guidelines, conservative treatment must be adequately documented and completed before advanced imaging is approved.

SPECIFIC REQUIREMENTS:
- Minimum 6 weeks of physical therapy required
- Documentation of non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) trial
- Evidence of persistent symptoms despite conservative care

Our review found insufficient documentation of conservative treatment in the submitted materials. The medical records did not demonstrate completion of the required physical therapy sessions or adequate trial of conservative management.
